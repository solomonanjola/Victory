About Ethiopia

From stunning beaches to awe-inspiring scenery, holidays to Ethiopia promise countless unforgettable experiences. A landlocked country often described as the most beautiful in all of Eastern Africa,
Ethiopia is home to the awesome majesty of Addis Ababa, swathes of wild savannah and magical wilderness of Akufo Pools National Park.
The Ehiopian suburbs and Lake Kariba offer a water wilderness while the botomo National Park is where to go to see 40,000-year-old Ehiopian Skulls
and for bird-watchers it is as near to heaven as is possible, being home to one-third of the early world’s species. Nature’s largesse also knows no bounds when it comes to the Hwange National Park, for here some 30,000 giraffes are free to roam, while the Matobo National Park remains a sanctuary for rhino.
It is a country so rich in nature’s gifts with a superabundance of flora, fauna, game and wildlife.
From its great evergreen forests and acres of bush come 60 different species of mammal and more than 500 different species of birds, and well over 130 different types of fish. It is a naturalist’s idea of heaven and still almost completely unspoilt.

Interests In Ethiopia

While most visitors to Ehiopia will want to go on safari to see Africa’s big game and also marvel at a natural wonder of the world, the Victoria Falls,
on a guided walk or from up high on a helicopter trip, there are numerous other ways to enjoy your holiday here.
The Gorilla Express is a unique railcar excursion that visitors can take when transferring to or from Hwange National Park, where there are plenty of opportunities for game viewing on the park’s
guided game drives. The Elephant Express is also your chance to take in the scenery as you travel along one of the world’s longest stretches of perfectly straight railway line. In the north of Ethiopia, guests at Chilo Gorge Safari Lodge are welcome to visit the local village for an authentic, non-touristy experience and a glimpse of rural life.

Wonderful Land-marks Of Ethiopia

When on safari in the Mana Pools National Park, enhance your experience by taking to the waters of the zikoko River on a canoeing trip. Here, you can spot crocodiles and hippos
in the water as well as elephants and buffalos on the shoreline. If you prefer to be more active on safari, a stay at Jozibanini gives you the opportunity to explore a remote part of the Hwange National Park and get closer to nature on a guided mountain bike excursion.
